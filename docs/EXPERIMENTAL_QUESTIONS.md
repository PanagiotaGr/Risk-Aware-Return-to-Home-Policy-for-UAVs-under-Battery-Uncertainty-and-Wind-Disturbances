# Experimental Questions

Status: **Research Prototype / Simulation-Only Evidence**.

- **RQ1:** Does probabilistic RTH reduce unsafe energy depletion compared with fixed battery thresholds?
- **RQ2:** Does modelling wind uncertainty improve return success under headwinds and gusts?
- **RQ3:** Does battery-health uncertainty change the optimal RTH trigger?
- **RQ4:** What is the trade-off between mission progress and safe-return probability?
- **RQ5:** Does sequential decision-making outperform a one-time binary continue/return decision?
- **RQ6:** Can alternate landing sites reduce catastrophic mission failures?
- **RQ7:** How sensitive are results to Monte Carlo sample count, risk threshold, battery noise, and wind-model error?
- **RQ8:** How well calibrated is the predicted safe-return probability?

## Reporting rules

All numerical claims must be generated from recorded runs with configuration, seed, software environment, command, and output paths. Monte Carlo outputs are estimates, not exact probabilities. Simulation results do not constitute flight certification, formal guarantees, or real-world safety validation.
