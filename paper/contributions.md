# Contribution Scaffold

## Implemented contributions

1. A transparent probabilistic RTH formulation based on safe-return probability.
2. A modular 2D simulator for controlled UAV RTH studies.
3. Battery, wind, and energy models with explicit uncertainty assumptions.
4. Monte Carlo safe-return estimation with confidence intervals.
5. Deterministic and probabilistic policy baselines under a common interface.
6. YAML-driven reproducibility, tests, and generated visualization scripts.

## Prototype contributions

- Demo GIF/MP4 generation from simulator traces.
- Threshold-sensitivity and calibration workflows through extensible scripts.

## Not yet claimed

- Real-world flight safety.
- Superiority on public UAV benchmarks.
- Hardware validation.
- Learned policies or formal guarantees.
